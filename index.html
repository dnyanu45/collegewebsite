Founder



Dr Ajeenkya DY Patil

Dr. Ajeenkya DY Patil is the President of Ajeenkya D Y Patil University and Chairman of Ajeenkya DY Patil Group. His influential leadership extends to several other organizations, including Vice President of Continental Medicare Foundation, Managing Director of Trancefx Studios, Vice President of DY Patil Educational Academy, and Trustee and Pro-Chancellor of Dr DY Patil University in Navi Mumbai. Dr Patil has achieved remarkable success throughout his career with an MBA from Richmond College, United Kingdom, and an Honorary Doctorate from the University of Central Lancashire, United Kingdom. He was appointed Chairman of the Central Board for Workers Education (CBWE) by the Ministry of Labour and Government of India. The President of Guyana, appointed him as the Economic Advisor. In July 2007 and April 2010, he was nominated as the Chancellor’s nominee on the Management. The Council of the University of Mumbai.
